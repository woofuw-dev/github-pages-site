Welcome to the Club

The Wisconsin Organization of Furries looks to provide a social and creative outlet for those interested in furry culture.

Who We Are:

Students & Alumni

We are students and alumni in the Madison, WI area.

(UW-Madison, MATC, and Edgewood)

Furries

We are people interested in furries and furry culture, or are looking to learn more about it!

LGBTQ+ & Allies

We are people who encourage self-expression and embracing our sexualities.

What the Dog(s) Doin'?

Conventions

We help organize and attend furry conventions!
(Anthrocon, MFF, FurSquared, etc.)

"What's better than being a fluffball for a weekend?"

Events

We host regular "Kickback" gatherings where we play games, draw, and more.

Our group also hosts gift-exchanges, lunch get-togethers, and plenty more activities!

How do I get involved?

Join our Discord! This is where we discuss events, communicate, and play games together!
